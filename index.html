💜 About BTS:

BTS (also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or Bulletproof Boy Scouts) is a South Korean boy band that debuted in 2013 under the company Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE). The group has 7 members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Their music mixes K-pop, hip-hop, R&B, and pop styles.

Their fandom is called ARMY, one of the biggest and most dedicated fan communities in the world. In short, BTS is more than just a music group—they are a global cultural phenomenon loved for their talent, teamwork, and inspiring messages.
